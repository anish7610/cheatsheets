Linux `bg` Command

Description

The bg command is used to resume a suspended job in the background.

Common Options

Option Description
bg <job_spec> Resumes a specific suspended job in the background.

Usage Examples 

# Suspend a job using Ctrl+Z
$ find / -name "*.log"

# Resume the most recent suspended job in the background
$ bg

# Resume a specific job in the background
$ bg %1

Cheat Sheet 

# Resume recent suspended job
bg

# Resume specific job
bg %n
